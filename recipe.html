Sample Recipes

Golden Hearth Dumplings

Mix flour and warm water to form a dough.
Knead until smooth, then let it rest for 30 minutes.
Combine meat, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, green onions, salt, and pepper.
Roll out the dough and cut into small circles.
Fill each with a spoonful of filling and fold into dumplings.
Fry dumplings until golden brown, then add a splash of water and cover to steam.
Serve hot with dipping sauce.
"A comforting embrace from home"
"A vibrant symphony of hearty flavors"

Lanzhou Ramen

Simmer beef with garlic, soy sauce, and Sichuan peppercorns for 1 hour.
Cook noodles separately in boiling water, then drain.
Assemble bowls with noodles, pour hot broth over, and top with beef slices.
Drizzle with chili oil, add green onions and cilantro.
"Slurp on a soothing bowl of hand-pulled perfection"
